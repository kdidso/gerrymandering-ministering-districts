Steps 3–7

District assignment, polygons, and claiming cells

Step 3 assigns districts by ordered latitude/longitude batches within each group, while keeping same-cell points together, then draws district areas and escape routes. Step 4 finalizes overlaps at the cell-mask level so older districts win and trapped foreign points can escape through carved corridors. Step 5 claims cells from those finalized district masks and also claims cells containing that district’s own points. Step 6 safely expands districts and step 7 fills all remaining cells.
