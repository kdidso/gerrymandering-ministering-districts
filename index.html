Gerrymandering Ministering Districts

This version builds districts from ward cells instead of network lines. Filter the ministering pool, compute district targets, then color districts and fill the whole ward by assigning every cell to one district. If two districts land in the same cell, that cell is temporarily split only for drawing and district assignment.

Step 3

Cell-based district generation

Points are colored into provisional districts first. Then cells are assigned and dissolved into closed district areas so the whole ward is filled. Same-cell conflicts are handled by temporary sub-cell splits only when needed.
